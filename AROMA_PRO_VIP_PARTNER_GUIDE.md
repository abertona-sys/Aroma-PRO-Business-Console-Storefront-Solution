Once orders arrive, your VIP suite upgrades your back-end workspace to a professional production level.

### 1. The Active Orders Registry
- Located under the **Active Orders** deck.

  - `Batch Completed`: The wax formulas have been poured, packaged, and shipped. Marking completed instantly routes transaction values to your corporate charts!
  - `Cancelled`: Order retired.

### 2. Raw Supply Tracking & Stock Log
Keep an organized count of waxes, oils, and packaging bases in the **Materials & Raw Stocks** deck:
- Enter item descriptions (e.g., *Golden Brands 464 Soy Wax*).
- Specify stock weight (e.g., *2500*) and units (*grams / ounces*).
- Track raw balances privately to schedule refills before raw shortages impact your client delivery pipelines.

### 3. The Aroma Batch Calculator (Scent Chemistry VIP Upgrade)
One of the highest-value tools for VIP builders, this professional calculator ensures perfectly saturated pours:
1. Input your intended **Scent Items to Pour** (e.g., *12 units*).
2. Enter the **Weight per Sachet/Melt** in grams or ounces (input *85g*).
3. Set your **Fragrance Percentage Load** (standard professional formulations sit at *10%*).
4. The system calculates your precise raw ratios using the strict formula:
   $$\text{Wax weight} = \frac{\text{Total Weight Needed}}{1 + \text{Fragrance Load Percentage}}$$
   $$\text{Fragrance Oil weight} = \text{Total Weight Needed} - \text{Wax weight}$$
5. The console shows exactly how many grams of clean wax to melt and how many grams of fragrance oil to blend, saving thousands in fragrance oil waste.
